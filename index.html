Artist Statement

Scientific ideas can be tender.

This portfolio merges crystal-cyberpunk atmosphere with interactive scientific systems, turning orbital mechanics, chaos, and recursion into inhabitable visual worlds.

I approach code as both structure and light: a medium for turning abstraction into sensation. Orbital systems, chaos attractors, interference patterns, and satellites are not only scientific objects to me — they are visual metaphors for intimacy, memory, danger, and scale.
